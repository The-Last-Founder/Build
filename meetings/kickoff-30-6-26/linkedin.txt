A spark was lit last night: Build [1] is becoming a hybrid of:



1. The default handbook for agentic building.

2. A live space for builders who don’t want to work alone with AI all day.

3. A quest board where work becomes visible and people can team up around it, and pick up rewards.



Yesterday’s kickoff combined connection, vision, and a live mockup of Quest Board, our pilot project. [2]



The field is fragmented right now: tools everywhere, agents everywhere, frameworks everywhere, but not enough shared practice for how people actually build AI-first products together.



Interesting timing: YC just published its latest Request for Startups, and the direction feels very aligned: AI-native companies, software for agents, company operating systems, and tools that own real outcomes instead of just generating text. [3]



Open for all skill levels, from zero to expert.
