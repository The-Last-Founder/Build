## Key Outcomes

The inaugural Build community meeting launched a shared vision for an open-source, learn-by-shipping platform where builders collaborate on real projects using AI agents. The group voted to prioritize building an **app mock-up** over a marketing website as the first demo artifact, with GitHub Copilot generating an initial HTML prototype live during the session. The pilot project selected is **QuestBoard** — a community hub where builders submit projects ranked by activity, stars, and revenue. 
## Decisions Made

- **Pilot project:** QuestBoard selected as the first community build — a leaderboard-style hub for submitting and ranking projects. 
- **Demo priority:** App mock-up chosen over static marketing site; rationale was that building the actual product is more exciting and skill-building. 
- **Open source default:** Pilot/community projects to be open source (MIT license); commercial projects may also participate with separate incentive tiers. 
- **Governance model:** Ron acts as **benevolent dictator for now** as initiator; leadership to evolve as community grows. 
- **Platform direction:** Likely migration from WhatsApp to **Slack** to enable native Claude/ClaudeTag integration.
